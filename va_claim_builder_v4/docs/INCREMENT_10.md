# Increment 10 — Literature, Specialist Packets, and Binder Export

This increment adds conservative medical-literature intake, specialist-specific nexus review packets, and a final ZIP binder with a manifest and SHA-256 provenance.

## Guardrails

- Literature is general medical background only and is never treated as patient-specific causation.
- Citation metadata and identifiers must be reviewed before inclusion.
- Clinician packets contain approved facts and questions, not a fabricated signed opinion.
- Binder export is for human final review and does not guarantee acceptance by VA systems.
