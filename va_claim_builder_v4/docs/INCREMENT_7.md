# Increment 7 — Claim Drafting Intelligence

This increment turns approved evidence into conservative, source-traceable drafting inputs.

## Added

- Approved Fact Matrix by claim element.
- Separation of medical, lay, military, and administrative authority.
- Draft assertion validation for unsupported dates, diagnoses, and lay medical conclusions.
- Review-draft generation for clinician nexus letters, personal statements, and buddy statements.
- Original uploaded draft text may be retained as the starting point.
- Every inserted factual proposition carries a source document and page citation.
- Negative or ambiguous approved evidence is included in a reconciliation section instead of being hidden.
- Clinician and witness certification workspaces.

## Guardrail

The generator does not make or sign a medical opinion. It prepares a reviewable draft using approved evidence and leaves the ultimate medical conclusion and rationale to the clinician.
