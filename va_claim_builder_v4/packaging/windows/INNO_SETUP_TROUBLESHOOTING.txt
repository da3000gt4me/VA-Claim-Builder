VA Claim Builder - Inno Setup detection

The required compiler is ISCC.exe from Inno Setup 6.

1. Confirm it exists:
   Get-ChildItem "C:\" -Filter ISCC.exe -File -Recurse -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue | Select-Object -First 10 FullName

2. If found in a custom location, set it for the current PowerShell window:
   $env:ISCC_PATH="C:\Path\To\ISCC.exe"

3. Re-run:
   powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File packaging\windows\build_windows.ps1

The warning does not invalidate the portable app at dist\VAClaimBuilder\VAClaimBuilder.exe.
