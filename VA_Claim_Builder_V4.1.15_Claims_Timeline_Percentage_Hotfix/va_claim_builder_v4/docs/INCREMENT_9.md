# Increment 9 — DBQ/C&P Intelligence, Evidence Development, and Assembly Validation

This increment adds conservative DBQ/C&P section parsing, a prioritized evidence-gap action planner, broader condition-profile coverage, and final package assembly validation. Extracted examination findings remain review-required and are never treated as verified merely because a heading or phrase was detected. Rating profiles are screening aids and must be checked against the current regulation and the veteran's actual medical findings.
