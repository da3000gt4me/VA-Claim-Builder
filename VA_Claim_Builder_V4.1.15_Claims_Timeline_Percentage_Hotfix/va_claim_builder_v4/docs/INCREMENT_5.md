# Increment 5 — Explainable Evidence Review and Claim-Element Autofill

This increment replaces keyword-oriented review with proposition-oriented review. Every suggested item states what fact it proves, why it supports a claim element, its limits, source authority, temporal context, and copy-forward risk.

## Added
- Explainable evidence cards and structured reviewer rationale.
- Claim-element coverage table by service-connection theory.
- Automatic suppression of repeated copy-forward passages.
- Pending auto-population only for affirmative, favorable, sufficiently scored, non-copy-forward findings.
- Editable reviewer fields before approval.
- Review decisions that preserve the original quote and source page.
